Caliby Cat
a distinct feline coat pattern that combines the three colors of a calico (white, orange, black/grey) with the striped, mottled patterns of a tabby.
Calico Cat
a domestic cat of any breed with a tri-color coat of black, white, and orange.
Tabby Cat
not a specific breed but describes any domestic cat with a coat pattern distinguished by an M-shaped marking on its forehead, stripes by its eyes, cheeks, along its back, and around its legs and tail.
